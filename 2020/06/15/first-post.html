Alright, here we go

This is probably the third/fourth blog I’m trying to run. It’s funny, because I didn’t even get to write my fourth post. My blogs just died out of my laziness. Funny, I just remembered the following (((LOSS))) posted in a Facebook group full of people pretending to be ants:

That’s pretty much it, fellas. Then we build it again.

So, my first urge was to put a lot of work into customizing this blog, but that’s pretty much useless if I don’t even get to write as many as 2 blog posts . So I’m Keeping It Simple, Stupid, at least until I manage to get some work done here. I’m not worrying about meta tags. I’m not worried about SEO. I’m not even worried about a fucking icon. I just want to write something. Buuut, I’m curious mind with ADHD, so expect me to play around with tinkles and twinkles of some blogging plugins that I may find somewhere (on GitHub). It’s funny, I’m writing all of this shit like anyone would care, which probably isn’t the case, but idc, because I have started the journey through my twenties learning to enjoy myself.

Have fun, take risks, but don’t lose your mind. Peace & love
